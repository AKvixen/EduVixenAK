# Email Setup for Edu-VixenAK Feedback System

## Recommended Email Setup

For your Edu-VixenAK project, you should create a dedicated email address for receiving feedback and support requests.

### Suggested Email Options:

1. **support@eduvixenak.com** (currently shown in the app)
2. **feedback@eduvixenak.com**
3. **contact@eduvixenak.com**
4. **help@eduvixenak.com**

### Email Provider Options:

#### Option 1: Gmail Business (Google Workspace)
- Cost: ~$6/month per user
- Benefits: Professional, reliable, integrates with Google services
- Setup: Sign up at workspace.google.com

#### Option 2: Free Gmail Account
- Cost: Free
- Benefits: Easy setup, reliable
- Example: eduvixenak.support@gmail.com
- Setup: Create at gmail.com

#### Option 3: Custom Domain Email
- Cost: Varies ($5-15/month)
- Benefits: Most professional, custom domain
- Providers: Namecheap, GoDaddy, Hostinger
- Example: support@eduvixenak.com

### Where to Update the Email Address:

Once you create your project email, update it in:
1. `client/src/components/SimpleFeedbackButton.tsx` (line 97)
2. Any contact pages or about sections
3. Documentation and README files

### Email Integration Features (Optional):

Later, you can enhance the feedback system to:
- Send email notifications when new feedback is submitted
- Auto-reply to feedback submissions
- Forward feedback to your email
- Send weekly feedback summaries
